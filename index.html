Quiet answers.

What most people ask first.

Does Alpha place trades for me?
No. The crew watches real markets and voices what it sees. It never places orders, never touches your broker. Every click is yours. That's the point of a companion, not an autopilot.
How is setup different from other AI trading tools?
Most tools want a download, an account, a broker wire-up, then payment. alphawalk lives in a chat you already have. Open Telegram, find @alphawalk, say hello. ~30 seconds. No new app. No new password.
What does alphawalk actually do for me?
Your crew watches the markets you care about and explains setups like a patient mentor. Voice or text — only when it matches your style. Over time you're not just getting signals. You're learning to read the market yourself.
Where does my data live?
Messages stay on your messenger, under its security. alphawalk stores only what it needs to run your crew: preferences, watchlist, prompt history. We don't sell or share data.
Is this investment advice?
No. alphawalk is an information and learning tool — nothing the crew says is a recommendation. Markets carry real risk. You decide. See full disclosures.
What does it cost?
Free during beta. No credit card. No 7-day-trial trick. Beta walkers keep a founding rate when paid tiers launch.
